How We Work

From hide to
hanger, in-house.

One factory floor, five checkpoints. You approve the sample; we run the line. No subcontracted stitching, no surprise substitutions.

01

Sourcing

Full-grain & suede hides sourced from regional tanneries, graded before cutting.

02

Pattern & Cut

In-house pattern room grades your spec sheet across a full size run.

03

Stitch & Build

Dedicated leather-line stitchers assemble, reinforce, and finish seams.

04

QC & Branding

Piece-by-piece inspection, then your labels, hangtags & packaging.

05

Ship

Consolidated air or sea freight, DDP or FOB, to your warehouse door.

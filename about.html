Our Story

A factory floor
in Dhaka.

Bangladesh has quietly become one of the world's largest leather and garment production hubs. RAWx LJ was built to put that capability directly in front of international brands — no middleman trading house, no marked-up agency layer.

Why We Started

Cut out the
middle layer.

Most international buyers sourcing leather jackets go through a trading company that never touches a hide — adding 30–50% before the order even reaches a factory. RAWx LJ is the factory. Our design, pattern-cutting, stitching and QC teams sit under one roof in Dhaka's Tejgaon industrial belt, so the price you're quoted is close to the real cost of production.

We work exclusively B2B: boutique owners building a first outerwear line, established labels adding leather to their range, and distributors stocking wholesale volume. No retail storefront, no single-unit orders — just consistent factory-direct supply.

How We Got Here

Timeline.

Y1

Small workshop, three stitching lines

Started as a contract manufacturer producing biker jackets for two regional retailers.

Y2

In-house pattern room built

Brought pattern grading and sample development in-house to cut sample turnaround from weeks to days.

Y3

First direct export orders

Began shipping factory-direct to buyers in Europe and North America, bypassing trading agents.

Y4

Private label division launched

Formalized OEM services — branded labels, hangtags and packaging on every bulk order.

Today

RAWx LJ — 22 countries served

Full-capacity leather jacket manufacturing for wholesale and private-label buyers worldwide.
